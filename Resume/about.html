Jose Rodriguez is a Mechanical Engineer with Finance focused MBA from Rice University; interested in technology and making a difference. His background as a well-rounded business professional includes both local and international work experience in multiple functions, spanning: Business Development & Strategy, Economic & Decision Analysis, Business Planning & Analysis, Corporate Treasury and Risk Management, Production and Manufacturing Engineering, and Organizational Design.

As an engineer his background includes multiple years of relevant experience in the roles of business & product development, process improvement consulting, capital project management, innovative reliability improvement, small robotics development, and plant optimization. He is an accomplished cross-functional engineer and manager who is an expert at effectively organizing and communicating as a leader from the office to the field, across multiple departments and organizations, and from conceptual engineering design to final product delivery.

As a data scientist my knowledge extends to Advanced Excel, Python 3 (Pandas Matplotlib, NumPy, API Interactions, Social Media Mining) JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, D3.js, Geomapping with Leaflet.js, SQL, MongoDB, ETL, Tableau, Advanced Statistics, Machine Learning, R, and Git/GitHub. My hobbies are more front end programming focusing on user experience.
